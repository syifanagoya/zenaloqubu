Who Does Benedict Marry in Bridgerton? Details From the Book Series

Penelope and Colin’s love story might be taking center stage in Bridgerton season 3, but we’re dying to know who Benedict will marry in the hit Netflix show.

Related story These Bridgerton Stars Are Rumored To Be Dating in Real Life

In season one, we watched as Daphne Bridgerton found love with Simon Basset. Season two was all about Anthony Bridgerton’s complicated love story with Kate Sharma. And now, amid all the frenzy over “Polin” — Penelope Featherington and Colin Bridgerton — there’s another Bridgerton sibling waiting to find his true love.

While Benedict Bridgerton’s love story is the focus of the third novel in Julia Quinn’s Bridgerton book series, Netflix opted for a different route. Instead of seeing book three, An Offer From a Gentleman, play out, Netflix’s third season is based on book four, Romancing Mister Bridgerton. Thus, the show has introduced us to a new love interest for Benedict in the form of Lady Tilley Arnold, a wealthy widow who he starts a physical relationship with. But, who will Benedict actually end up with?

Who does Benedict marry in Bridgerton?

In Julia Quinn’s book series, Benedict Bridgerton marries Sophie Beckett, the daughter of an Earl who ends up working for the Bridgerton family, in Cinderella-like love story that fans are hoping plays out in season four of the Netflix show.

However, the show is known to deviate from the books so who knows what direction Benedict, who is played by Luke Thompson, will take in his quest for love on-screen.

Benedict Bridgerton marries Sophie Beckett at the end of the third ‘Bridgerton’ book.

In An Offer From a Gentlemen, Benedict becomes entranced by a mystery woman who attends a masquerade ball hosted by the Bridgertons. She exits the ball, leaving behind a glove, before Benedict can catch her name. He becomes infatuated and vows to find her. What he doesn’t know is that Sophie is the illegitimate daughter of an Earl who is forced to be a servant to her stepmother and stepsisters after her father’s death.

Two years after Benedict first meets Sophie, a skew of circumstances lead to her becoming Violet Bridgerton’s maid, all without Benedict realizing she’s the woman he met at the ball.

The pair form a romantic connection all while Benedict is still hoping to reunite with the woman from the ball. Eventually (thankfully) he puts two and two together, realizing Sophie is the woman he has been looking for all along.

They marry and go on to welcome four children together: Charles, Alexander, William, and Violet.

Will Benedict and Sophie marry in Bridgerton season four?

So far, Sophie and Benedict’s love story has not began in the Netflix adaptation of the show. However, fans have speculated that we may be introduced to Sophie in the final episodes of season three. In season three, Anthony tells Eloise that she needs to hire a new maid, which fans suspect could end up being Sophie, though this would differ from the plot of the original book.

According to one fan on TikTok, paparazzi photos taken during the filming of the season three finale, showed a new maid getting into a carriage with one of the Bridgerton sisters.

Fans believe this maid is South African actress Masali Baduza who could be playing Sophie next season.

Bridgerton showrunners have not confirmed whether Bridgerton season four will focus on Benedict and Sophie but they have been open about altering details from Quinn’s books.

“We already differ from the books a little bit because we’re an ensemble show,” showrunner Jess Brownell told Variety in May 2022 after it was revealed that Benedict’s love story would not appear in season three. “The books really focus on one on one romantic pair at a time and maybe the siblings have a little cameo here and there but they don’t have full stories. In that way, even though we’re reversing the order of the books, I think all the people you would expect to see — Benedict, who is book three, he will be a vital part of Season 3.”

She also hinted that some elements of the siblings’ love stories could be altered to fit into plans to have eight seasons. “I think [Bridgerton creator] Shonda Rhimes has said publicly before that she wants to have eight seasons. We hope to get to tell every sibling’s love story and they will always be in each season.”
