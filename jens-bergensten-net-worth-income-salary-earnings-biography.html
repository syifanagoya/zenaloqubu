Jens Bergensten Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Jens Peder Bergensten, commonly known as Jeb, or jeb_ in-game, is a Swedish video game creator who works in the genre of role-playing games. Since December 2010, he has been employed as a programmer and game designer at the video game company Mojang Inc. After Markus “Notch” Persson resigned from his role as lead designer and lead developer of the famous indie sandbox game Minecraft in December 2011, he took over as the game’s lead designer and lead developer.
