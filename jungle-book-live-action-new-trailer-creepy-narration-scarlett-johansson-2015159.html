Jungle Book's New Trailer Has Creepy Narration From Scarlett Johansson

It clearly took more than the “bear necessities” to make this film into a reality! The first trailer has been released for Disney’s upcoming live-action, CGI-enhanced Jungle Book movie, and it’s about as fantastical and exciting as you’d imagine. 

But what some fans might not be prepared for is how creepy the trailer feels, thanks to the narration by a spooky Scarlett Johansson, who plays the snake, Kaa. 

“Are you alone out here? What are you doing so deep in the jungle?” she asks at the beginning of the clip.

When it finally comes time for the serpent to show herself, she is massive, lording over the young Mowgli, flicking her tongue out in a menacing fashion. 

But that doesn’t mean there’s not a little trace of the feel-good original we know and love. At the very end of the 2-minute teaser clip, we see Mowgli sitting on top of the bear Baloo’s (voiced by Bill Murray) stomach as he floats down the river singing “The Bare Necessities.”
