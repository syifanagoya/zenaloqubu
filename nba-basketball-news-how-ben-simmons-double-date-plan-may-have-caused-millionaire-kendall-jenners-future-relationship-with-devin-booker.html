How Ben Simmons Double Date Plan May Have Caused Millionaire Kendall JennersFuture Rela

Over the years, NBA players have dated some of the top celebrities in the world. NBA stars are some of the premier and most popular athletes in the world. So obviously they are star attractions. But one story on how a double date plan made by Ben Simmons may have caused Kendall Jenner and Devin Booker to date will blow your minds.

Back in 2018, Kendall and Simmons were in a relationship. The duo had met through mutual friends and were going strong. Jenner attended Philadelphia 76ers games while Simmons played for the franchise. But then Simmons made a plan which could have caused his breakup and even planned his girlfriend’s future relationship with an All-Star.

Simmons and Jenner started dating back in 2018, but they were on and off several times. But in 2018, when they were going strong, Simmons took Jenner to dinner with his long-time friend and fellow NBA star Devin Booker and his girlfriend.

This was in 2018 and in 2020, Simmons and Jenner officially called it quits.

Simmons’ double date that may have led to Devin Booker and Kendall Jenner

And reportedly this double date is what caused them to become friends, and remained in touch since then.
