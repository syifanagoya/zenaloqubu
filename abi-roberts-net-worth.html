FAQs about Abi Roberts

Who is Abi Roberts?

Abi Roberts is a Welsh stand-up comedian and political commentator known for her vibrant presence on the UK comedy circuit and media platforms like GB News and Good Morning Britain.

When did she start her comedy career?

Roberts became a professional stand-up comedian in 2012 and has since performed at major clubs across the UK, including several sold-out shows at the Edinburgh Festival Fringe.

What is she known for?

Beyond her stand-up comedy, Roberts is recognized for her work as a writer and commentator, with appearances on BBC Radio 4, ITV’s Good Morning Britain, and regular commentary on GB News.

Has Abi Roberts faced any controversies?

In June 2021, Roberts claimed that her agent dropped her for being a trans-exclusionary radical feminist (TERF), highlighting her outspoken nature on sensitive issues.

What are some of her significant achievements?

Notably, in February 2016, she became the first UK comic to perform in Russia, and in Russian, marking a historic moment in her comedy career.

How does she contribute to discussions on free speech and media?

Roberts is an active voice in discussions surrounding truth, liberty, free speech, and the pursuit of justice, often engaging with these topics in her daily podcast and articles on Substack.
