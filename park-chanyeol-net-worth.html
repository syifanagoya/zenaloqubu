What is Park Chanyeol's Net Worth?

Park Chanyeol is a South Korean rapper, singer, songwriter, and actor who has a net worth of $8 million. Park Chanyeol was born in Seoul, South Korea in November 1992. In 2012 he debuted as part of the boy band EXO as well as the sub-band EXO-K. EXO released their debut studio album XOXO in 2013 and it reached #1 in Korea and on the US World chart. Their albums EXODUS in 2015 and EX'ACT in 2016 both reached #1 in Korea #1 and #2 respective on the US World chart.

Chanyeol has been featured on the popular singles "Let Me Love You" (with Junggigo), "Stay With Me" (with Punch) and more. He has also written and composed many songs. As an actor Chanyeol starred in the 2016 film So I Married an Anti-fan. He has also starred in episodes of the TV series Law of the Jungle. Chanyeol has won two Korea Drama Awards for Exo Next Door and a Top Chinese Music Award for Most Popular Foreign Idol.
