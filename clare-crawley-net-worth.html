Clare Crawley Net Worth

Clare Crawley has an estimated net worth of $800,000. This is from her career an American television personality and hairstylist.
Crwaley  is best known for her role as the runner-up on the 18th season of The Bachelorette. She also appeared on seasons 1 and 2 of Bachelors in Paradise, and on Bachelor Winter Games.

After the Bachelor, Crawley appeared in the first two seasons of Bachelors in Paradise; however, she was unsuccessful in finding love and left voluntarily in both seasons.

Crawley was announced as The Bachelorette on March 2, 2020, on Good Morning America. This appearance makes her the oldest Bachelorette in the show’s history, taking over from Rachel Lindsay who was 32 on her season.
