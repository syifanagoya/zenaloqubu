Kevin Spacey Career

Spacey started his career by doing small roles in theater. As his parents were involved in a stage play he was attracted to the art form from childhood.

He appeared in Boardway plays like Ghosts, The Misanthrope and Hurlyburly, Sleuth, and The Seagull.

Spacey then tried his luck in television through the show called Crime Stories.

He then instantly got hook on the newfound art form and started foraying into movies.

Even though acting in minor roles before, Spacey’s major film role came in the movie See no Evil, Hear No Evil which was released in 1989.

He then didn’t need to turn back as he started appearing in critically and commercially successful movies.

Some of his high grossing movies are Superman Returns, A Bug’s Life, American Beauty, Se7en, Baby Driver, and Horrible Bosses.

His most memorable character was a serial killer named Jon Doe in the movie Se7en.

His character in Se7en has been a cult classic and still remains the most iconic antagonist in movies.

Kevin Spacey Controversy & Sexual Allegations

Spacey’s life hasn’t been aloof from controversies, he was constantly in news for several allegations made against him.

A 14-year-old boy accused Spacey of making sexual advances towards him in 1986 when Spacey was 26 years old.

The news broke in 2017 and Spacey was instantly criticized for his behavior.

Spacey in his defense blamed alcohol for the happening and also did came out as gay in a public statement.

More news started to pour into the surface of similar sexual misconduct from 15 different people, all pointing their fingers against Spacey.

These allegations had a deep impact on his career as well.

His breakout performance in the television series House of Cards was shortened to just eight episodes due to the allegations.

Also, many movies were later reshot removing the part of Spacey where he had already acted.

The movie which Spacey had already signed also went to other actors.

Similarly, his movie releasing also faced several backlashes.

The situation got worse when in 2019 total of 3 people who had ties with Spacey in the sexual allegation died.

All the cases of sexual assault and misconduct are closed with no major penalty for the actor.
