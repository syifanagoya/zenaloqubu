Anthony Ross “Tony” Katz (born 1972) is a political analyst, author, and radio presenter from the United States of America. Tony Katz Tonight, a daily radio programme broadcast on 93.1FM WIBC in Indianapolis, is hosted by him. The Tony Katz Show on FM News Talk 97.1FM in St. Louis was his previous job, and he was the host of that show. In addition, he presented a number of daily programmes for the All Patriots Media Network. TownHall.com and ChristianPost.com are among the websites where Tony contributes, and his writing has also featured on Breitbart.com and The Daily Caller. As a contributor to FOX News, MSNBC, CNBC, and a variety of radio stations throughout the nation, he provides political and election coverage and analysis.
