Nkiru Sylvanus Actress

Nkiru is a veteran Nollywood starlet who has been acting for a couple of years. She is one of those historians who created a niche for others to follow and one pillar that holds Nollywood to the ground.

She has created more than a hundred and fifty movies, starting as major and minor lead roles. Some of her common movies include: Cry for Help, Egg of Life, Tears of Vengeance, Mirror of Beauty, and many more others.

Nkiru Sylvanus Politician

In 2011 Nkiru was included in the cabinet of former governor of Imo state Rochas Okorocha as his Special Assistant on Lagos State Affairs and later became his Special Adviser on Public Affairs.

Nkiru Sylvanus Kidnapping

Nkiru, who was a special assistant to former Governor of Imo State, Rochas Okorocha on Public affairs was kidnapped on December 15, 2012, at 2.30 pm as reported by Nigerian media houses.

A Vanguard media publication reported that a ransom of One hundred million nairas which was (per exchange rate) in 2012 equivalent to Six Hundred and forty thousand U.S dollars was demanded by her captors in exchange for her release. It was reported by Nigerian media houses that on December 21, 2012, at 10:30 pm, Nkiru was released from her kidnappers.
